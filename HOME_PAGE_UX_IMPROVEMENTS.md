## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

1. **Above the Fold = Gold**
   - Show value proposition immediately
   - Make CTA visible without scrolling
   - Display trust signals early

2. **Reduce Friction**
   - Fewer form fields
   - One-click WhatsApp booking
   - Instant price estimates

3. **Build Trust Fast**
   - Real customer photos
   - Verified reviews
   - Transparent pricing
   - Clear warranties

4. **Create Urgency**
   - Limited slots
   - Time-bound offers
   - Live activity feed
   - Countdown timers

5. **Mobile First**
   - Thumb-friendly buttons
   - Sticky CTAs
   - Quick actions
   - Minimal scrolling
